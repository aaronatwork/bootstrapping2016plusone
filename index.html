What is Urban Biodiesel?

There's no magic bullet for a cleaner, greener future. While electric cars powered by solar, wind, and geothermal energy are on the horizon, diesel generators and fleet vehicles are in operation now and need more fuel options. Urban Biodiesel meets that need head-on by diverting waste streams from the urban environment as raw material for diesel fuel.
